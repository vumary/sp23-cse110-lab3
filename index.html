Recap of last meeting

Last meeting we were brainstorming project ideas that are do-able in the span of one summer. We wanted a project that would force us to learn as well as a project that would be practical. Eventually, we decided to agree on the idea of a pomodoro app. Even though many already exist, we want to make a pomodoro app with our own unique twist.

Agenda for today:

  1. Brainstorm ideas to make our app unique
  2. Create a Github repo and add all members
  3. Create a Figma and add all members
